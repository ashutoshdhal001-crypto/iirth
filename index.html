Institutional Enrollment Frameworks

IIRT maintains definitive selection filters across its UG, PG, and Doctoral tracks to maintain elite academic rigor:

UG (B.Tech Programs)

Strict filter limit: Candidates must secure an All India Rank from Rank 1 to Rank 100 in JEE Advanced.

PG (M.Sc / M.Tech Programs)

M.Sc allocations are administered strictly through the IIT-JAM examination cutoff channels. M.Tech tracks require valid GATE metrics.

Ph.D. Tracks (Current Cycle Active)

Applicants must possess valid National Fellowship scores: CSIR-UGC NET-JRF or top-tier GATE scores in respective sciences.
